রংতুলি

রংতুলি, located in Dhaka, is dedicated to showcasing the artistry of individuals. Providing them with a platform to both create and sell their products. By providing both skills and opportunities, we wish to uplift the community — making sure everyone lives with dignity and purpose.

Learn More About Our Mission

What We Provide

  1. Training: We will appoint an experienced tailor and provide them with a salary. They will conduct 6-month training courses, teaching people from diverse backgrounds tailoring, stitching, and designing skills. Our goal is to ensure that every trainee becomes capable of earning independently by the end of the program.
  2. Funding: Through donations, micro-loans, and community contributions, we will offer financial support to trainees. This funding will help them purchase the materials and the tools needed to start their own small-scale work.
  3. Resources: We will provide access to machinery, workspace, delivery platforms, and other necessary resources so that trainees can focus on learning and producing without worrying about financial or logistical barriers.
  4. Online Platform: An online platform will be created to connect sellers (the trainees) directly with buyers. This platform will showcase their products and services, helping them reach a wider market and grow sustainably.

What They Provide

  1. Ability: We will train individuals in skills like stitching, tailoring, and fashion design to help them start their own service or small business. This can be conducted in schools after class hours, providing a safe environment for training.
  2. Computing: Providing hands-on computer training for youths so they can update stock and pricing efficiently.
  3. Accounting: Offering practical lessons in bookkeeping and small business accounting, enabling trainees to manage finances efficiently.
  4. Delivery Services: Creating further employment opportunities through logistics and delivery networks — training individuals in delivery systems, customer handling, and basic e-commerce operations.
